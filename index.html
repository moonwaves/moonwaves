F.A.Q.Frequently asked questions

When is the mint date?

Minting started 18 december.
Minting price per NFT is 1 SOL.

How and where do I mint?

You will be able to mint on the following url: https://mint.moonwaves.art

Connect your wallet and then you will be able to see a Mint button. You can mint as many as you want as long as there are NFTs still available.

What are the different types of attributes and traits?

There are 10 different traits with over 40 different attributes.

Color Palette Moon Color Stars Tide Gradient Gradient palette Wave Type Wave Style Effects Border

The complete rarity chart will be available after mint.

How do I generate the high resolution file of my NFT?

Shortly after mint, you will be able to login with your Solana wallet of choice.

Then you will be provided with a download link for every Moon Wave you have in your wallet.

300 DPI, CMYK, print-ready .tif file.

You will be able to download your high resolution NFT on the following url: https://print.moonwaves.art

What dimensions can I print?

The most popular frame size is 19¾ x 27½ inch / EU: 50x70 cm.

Moon Waves will fit perfectly in those types of dimensions.

Is there a limit on how many I can mint?

No, there is no limit. You can mint as many as you want.
